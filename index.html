Life Ministries
Our mission is to equip believers to live life to the fullest (John 10:10). Life Ministries Valdosta provides an alternative to traditional counseling and coaching through Christ-centered teaching and healing through Life Mentoring and guidance through Life Coaching, as well as training that equips the body of Christ to meet the needs of those who are hurting through Life Training.

Life Mentoring

Life Mentors believe that the Bible is true and sufficient for addressing all of life’s problems that are not organic in nature, and that reliance on the Holy Spirit for the healing process is essential. Therefore, Life Mentors use the Bible as their primary tool in bringing a person’s concerns to resolution while relying on the Holy Spirit for the results. Our mentors guide individuals into God’s truth and teach our true identity in Christ that individuals may find freedom and joy with Christ as our life. (Galatians 2:20) Life Mentors have been trained by certified trainers of the Network 220.

You must take responsibility for reaching your personal goals and living life to the fullest. This is a life-long growth journey that happens best in healthy community. Life Training

If you are interested in Life Training, read the requirements and syllabus for Life Class I and Life Class II, then contact Polly Barcol, Life Ministries Training Director, to sign up and pay your first payment. Once your down payment ($100.00) is paid, you will be given the password to access Life Class pages. Ask those who believe in your calling to help support you! Print as many copies as you need of the Support letter for classes and send to potential supporters.

While attending the Life Classes online, you will be able to find all the books, audio, and video that is required on this website or at The Life Bookstore, or check them out or view them at the Life Ministries Valdosta, GA office. You will need to set up an account at our video hosting site, Vimeo to watch the session videos. Send any questions to Polly Barcol.

Life Training

Life Ministries also offers classes to further teach the freeing principles covered in Life Mentoring. These classes are open to individuals who want to train to be a Life Mentor or just want to learn more about how to live the Exchanged Life. Students who complete the Life Training will receive a certificate recognized by the national organization, Network 220. Students can complete the training in a year and typically complete the training in this order:

01. Life Seminar

Life Seminar is a one day event.

02. Life Class I

Life Class I is four months weekly.

03. Life Class II

Life Class II is four months weekly.

04. Life Mentoring Internship

Life Mentoring Internship is two months approximately.

About Life Ministries

Life Ministries was founded in 2007 by Polly Barcol who began Life Mentoring individuals. Life Coaching was integrated in 2011. Our offices are located in Valdosta, GA, but we are not limited to this area. We offer our services through phone and video conferencing, so anyone can receive help and training no matter where you are.
