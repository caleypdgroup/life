About Life Ministries

Life Ministries was founded in 2007 by Polly Barcol who began Life Mentoring individuals. Life Coaching was integrated in 2011. Our offices are located in Valdosta, GA, but we are not limited to this area. We offer our services through phone and video conferencing, so anyone can receive help and training no matter where you are.
